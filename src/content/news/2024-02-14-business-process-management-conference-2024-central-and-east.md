**KAJ**: 22nd Business Process Management Conference 2024 – Central and Eastern Europe (CEE) Forum
**KDAJ**: 3. do 5. september 2024
**KJE**: Krakow, Poljska

Vabljeni k oddaji prispevkov na dogodek z mednarodnim organizacijskim odborom in udeležbo “**BPM Central and Eastern Europe (CEE) Forum**“, ki poteka pod okriljem **BPM 2024 – 22nd Business Process Management Conference, 2024**. Rok za oddajo povzetkov prispevkov je **21. maj 2024**.

Organizatorji dogodka:

- **_Gregor Polančič_**_, Univerza v Mariboru_
- **_Monika Malinova Mandelburger_**_, TU Wien_
- **_Katarina Tomičić-Pupek_**_, University of Zagreb_
- **_Michal Krčál_**_, Masaryk Universit_y

**Krovni dogodek**: [https://bpm2024.agh.edu.pl/](https://bpm2024.agh.edu.pl/)
**Povezava do CfP**: [https://bpm2024.agh.edu.pl/call-for-cee-forum/](https://bpm2024.agh.edu.pl/call-for-cee-forum/)
