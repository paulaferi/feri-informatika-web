---
The Robotics Interest Group at the Institute of Informatics is opening its doors to new members for the upcoming academic year. The group brings together students passionate about robotics, embedded systems and autonomous vehicles, meeting weekly to work on collaborative projects. This year's focus will be on building an autonomous indoor navigation platform using ROS 2 and a custom sensor array. No prior robotics experience is required — enthusiasm and a willingness to learn are the only prerequisites. Applications can be submitted through the student portal until 30 April 2025.
