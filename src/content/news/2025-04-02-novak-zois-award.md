The Slovenian Research and Innovation Agency has awarded Prof. Marko Novak the prestigious Zois Recognition for outstanding scientific achievements by a researcher under 40. The award recognises his contributions to the field of explainable artificial intelligence, in particular his work on interpretable neural network architectures for medical decision support systems. Prof. Novak is the youngest recipient of this recognition in the computer science category in the award's history. The ceremony took place at the Grand Hall of the University of Ljubljana on 29 March. The institute congratulates Prof. Novak on this well-deserved honour.
