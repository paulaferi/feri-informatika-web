Evropska univerza ATHENA vabi študente in druge na niz predavanj ATHENA Soft Skills Academy . Predavanja bodo zajemala tematike s področja »mehkih veščin« ter raziskovanja. Ta ponedeljek, 28. 3. 2022 , se bo odvilo že drugo predavanje, ko bo prof. Ana Barata predavala o veščinah sodelovanja (angl. collaboration skills).

V nadaljevanju sledi preliminarni program prihodnjih predavanj.

- Time Management Skills (21/03/2022) – K. Petridis (HMU, Greece)
- Collaboration SKills (28/03/2022) – Ana Barata (IPP, Portugal)
- Leadership Skills (04/04/2022) – Mario Caporale (Italian Space Agency, Italy)
- Resilience Skills (11/04/2022) – Kleio Koutra (HMU, Greece)
- Emotional Intelligence (02/05/2022) – TBA
- Cultural Intellingence (09/05/2022) – Tatjana Welzer (UM, Slovenia)
- Active Listening Skills (16/05/2022) – Ana Barata (IPP, Portugal)
- Presentation Skills (23/05/2022) – TBA
- How to prepare a poster and read a scientific report/paper (30/05/2022) – K. Petridis (HMU, Greece)
- How to build your Interview Skills (06/06/2022) – Maria Kartalou (THENAMARIS, Greece)
- How to perform bibliographic Research (13/06/2022) – Pietro Oliva (UNC, Italy)
- Building your Digital Skills (20/06/2022) – Pietro Oliva (UNC, Italy)
- Open Access to Research Publications – Reflection of a Librarian (27/06/2022) – Dunja Legat (UM, Slovenia)
- Reading Data on Research Funding and the Changing Role of Bibliometrics (04/07/2022) – Joszef Gyorkos (UM, Slovenia)
