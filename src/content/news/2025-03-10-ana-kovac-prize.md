---
Prof. **Ana Kovač** has received the National Research Prize in Informatics for her long-standing contributions to distributed systems research. The ceremony took place in Ljubljana in March. Prof. Kovač has published over 30 indexed articles and led multiple internationally funded projects over her 15-year career at the institute.
