A research team led by Prof. Ana Kovač has published a paper titled *Adaptive Routing Protocols for Energy-Constrained IoT Sensor Networks* in Nature Scientific Reports. The paper presents a novel routing algorithm that reduces energy consumption in dense sensor deployments by up to 34% compared to existing state-of-the-art methods. The work is part of the ongoing Horizon Europe project on industrial sensor networks and represents a significant milestone for the institute's international research profile. The paper is available open-access at the journal's website.
