The institute has received **Horizon Europe** funding for the project *Intelligent Sensor Networks for Industry 4.0*. Prof. **Marko Novak** will lead the team alongside Prof. **Ana Kovač**. The project runs from 2025 to 2027 and involves partners from Austria, Germany and Slovenia.
