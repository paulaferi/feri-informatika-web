title: "Institute Partners with Telekom Slovenije on 5G Research Initiative"
date: 2025-03-05T09:00:00.000Z
tags: ["professional", "project"]
summary: "The institute has signed a two-year research agreement with Telekom Slovenije to investigate 5G network optimisation using machine learning."
---
The Institute of Informatics has entered into a formal research partnership with Telekom Slovenije, Slovenia's leading telecommunications provider. The two-year agreement focuses on applying machine learning techniques to optimise 5G network resource allocation and reduce latency in high-density urban environments. The collaboration will involve three senior researchers and two PhD students from the institute, with fieldwork conducted at Telekom's infrastructure sites in Maribor and Ljubljana. This partnership is part of the institute's broader strategy to strengthen ties with the national technology industry.
