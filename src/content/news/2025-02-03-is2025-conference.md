---
The Institute of Informatics was proud to host the 28th edition of the Information Society Conference (IS 2025) at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, Computer Science and Informatics. Over 200 researchers from 18 countries attended the three-day event, presenting work across tracks including artificial intelligence, distributed systems, human-computer interaction and cybersecurity. The conference proceedings will be published in the IEEE Digital Library. The organising committee thanks all participants, reviewers and sponsors for making the event a success.
