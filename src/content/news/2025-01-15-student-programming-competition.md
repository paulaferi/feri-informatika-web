title: "Student Team Wins National Programming Competition"
date: 2025-01-15T10:00:00.000Z
tags: ["student", "awards"]
summary: "A team of three IPT students from FERI took first place at the national programming competition held in Ljubljana."
---
A team of three students from the Information Technologies programme represented FERI at the national programming competition in Ljubljana, competing against 24 teams from universities across Slovenia. Their project, a real-time flood monitoring system built with IoT sensors and a machine learning backend, was awarded first place by the jury. The students — Jana Horvat, Luka Peterlin and Miha Zorko — are now invited to represent Slovenia at the Central European Programming Contest in Vienna this autumn.
