---
title: "Horizon Europe Project on Industrial AI Receives Funding Extension"
date: 2025-03-18T10:00:00.000Z
tags: ["project", "scientific"]
summary: "The institute's Horizon Europe project on AI-driven industrial sensor networks has been granted a 12-month extension and additional funding of €180,000."
---
The European Research Executive Agency has approved a 12-month extension and supplementary funding of €180,000 for the institute's Horizon Europe project *Intelligent Sensor Networks for Industry 4.0*. The extension was granted following an excellent mid-term evaluation, in which the project received the highest possible score across all assessment criteria. Principal investigator Prof. Ana Kovač expressed her gratitude to the project team and international partners, noting that the additional resources will allow the team to conduct a large-scale pilot deployment at two industrial sites in Slovenia and Austria. The extended project runs until December 2026.
